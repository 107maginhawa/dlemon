cephalometric tracing feature (patient "Miguel Torres").

⚠ **ShareAlike caveat:** CC BY-SA 4.0 requires attribution and that derivatives
be shared under a compatible license. This is fine for an internal dev/demo seed
asset. Before redistributing this image in a shipped/customer-facing build,
replace it with a properly-licensed clinical image (or your own), or confirm
SA compatibility.

The seed places anatomically-estimated landmarks on this image; a clinician
drags-to-refine them in the workspace (the intended correction workflow).
